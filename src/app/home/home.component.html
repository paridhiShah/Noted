Who we are

This is an Educational Website that provides ample functionality and content. Noted bridges the gap created by online classes between students and teachers.

  • Based on Christ University syllabus
  • Notes, Question paper, Syllabus, conversation portal. All in one place!
  • Simpler and less time-consuming by providing a web-portal for students of the CS Department

The primary beneficiaries will be the students. It facilitates better management of study and support material for the students hence reducing the time that is wasted on collection of material. It aims at providing notes, syllabus, previous year question papers, project ideas etc.
